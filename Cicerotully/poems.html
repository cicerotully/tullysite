1.2 Daughter of Poseidon


A Licentious girl
Maiden of my destiny
Filled with propensity for knowledge
A talent for corruptibility

She will sit
Waiting by the corner of the bay
Where we always meet
A long walk
But not too far away

And when I'm late...

As sesquipedalian
She is cleverer than me
Driving words deep into the sand
Carving with bloodlust
Irascible feats in her love lust
As she
Driven mad
Barks with lost magnanimity
The fury of her words
Set to burn,
Warm winds
Trace the currents course
And she crashes threateningly on the shore

And as I hear a storm
Brew in her
Distrust
I'll hurry up

Salty
Breezy
Dust...

I'll arrive
And until Dusk we can stay there
Reading the stars in this mayhem we've created

And the rhythm of the sea
As the storm clouds gather
Mark the balance in our depravity
Greyer by greyer
By Blacker cloud
And I can't breathe
Or see the blue
In the sky or her eyes

A dream or nightmare
Never-ending nonetheless

We're as immortal as eternity
Wiling in the onerous vastness of this moment...

As I
Take watch
In the night
Over you
Sleeping on the strand
Between where dreams and reality
Surface at one time
And hold hands

Where onlookers breathe
A free chorus in the drizzle
And write us a lease
So we don't turn fickle

And I don't leave

The daughter of Poseidon

1.1 What She's Worth

01
To feel unwanted and abandoned
To feel that you have been disgraced
By the one you'd bear your heart to
Whose smile you've written on your face
02
To feel you couldn't bear another
Dagger, arrow, sword or spear
To want to hide your heart from danger
& yet to want to draw her near
03
To feel you need to let her go
And to just not have the strength -
or will to cast away the sorrows
But still, to want not to be helped
04
To wait and not get tired of waiting
Although time is slowly running out
And to breathe, and hope, and wish for more
Than love ever has poured out
05
To learn the discipline of acting -
acting fine when you are down
And though she gets you weeping more than she makes you smile -
to still want to work things out
06
To learn to speak the language
She signals silently to you
& to be fluent in every flinch and sigh
That her body throws at you
07
To seek to be her greatest friend
& her trusted council too
To want to be her sentinel -
the rock she holds on to
08
& to love her more than comfort
Oh, to want to hold her heart
To want to counter every blow
That sets out to hit its mark
09
To want to wrestle more with fate
Than you've ever dared before
To risk your heart and soul, your mind
Is what she's worth - and more
10
If that's what she is worth to you, at least
Well, then she's worth the World
If you can stand to let her dig that deep
She's your woman, she's your girl.
